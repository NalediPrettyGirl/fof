ABOUT US

Our Mission

Flames of Fire Church exists to ignite passion for God by making disciples who love God, grow in community, and serve the world. We are committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach.

Our Vision

Our vision is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ to all who are lost and bound by the chains of Satan. We aim to make disciples of all nations and lead them to their God-given destiny through Christ’s love, healing, and deliverance.

Learn More

OUR COMMUNITY

Our church family is diverse and welcoming. We believe in building meaningful relationships that support and encourage one another in faith and life.
