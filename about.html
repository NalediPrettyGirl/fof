About Us

Building a legacy of faith, hope, and transformation.

APOSTLE ALFRED MAKHAVHU

Apostle Alfred Makhavhu was born in 1965 in Diepkloof, Soweto. A dedicated servant of God, he has spent decades establishing order, excellence, and integrity within the Body of Christ.

Ordained in 1988, he served as a youth pastor before founding Flames of Fire Ministries in 1995. His vision has grown from a humble beginning in Rockville to a thriving community with branches in Zakariyya Park, Mofolo, and beyond.

Beyond the pulpit, Apostle Makhavhu founded the Christ Kingdom Missions in 2004 and established the Flames of Fire Bible School, empowering future leaders with biblical wisdom and accredited theological training.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The Trinity

We believe in one God, Creator of all things, existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ is God the Son, born of a virgin, died for our sins, and rose again.

Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, our ultimate authority for faith and living.

Salvation

We believe in the second birth through the Holy Spirit as the only way to reconciliation with God.

Second Coming

We believe in the visible, second coming of Jesus Christ to the earth as He promised in His Word.

Head of the Church

We believe that Jesus Christ is the sole Head of the universal church, which is His Body.
