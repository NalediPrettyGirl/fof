OUR FOUNDER

Apostle Alfred Makhavhu

Apostle Makhavhu was born in 1965 in Diepkloof, Soweto in a family of six children. He married Bongi Makhavhu and is blessed with two sons. As a faithful servant of the Lord, he strives to establish order, excellence and integrity in the Body of Christ.

Ordained in 1988, he founded Flames of Fire Ministries in Rockville Soweto in 1995. Under his ministry, the church has expanded significantly, establishing major branches in Zakariyya Park and across Gauteng. He also founded the Christ Kingdom Missions and the Flames of Fire Bible School, accredited by Teamwork Bible College International.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Statement of Faith

One God in Three Persons

We believe in one God, Creator of all things, existing in three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ - God the Son

We believe that Jesus Christ is God the Son, born of the virgin, died on the cross for our sins and rose again.

The Second Birth

We believe in the second birth through the Holy Spirit.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the Word of God and all Scriptures, both Old and New Testament are inspired by God.

The Second Coming

We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ to the earth as He promised.

Christ as Head

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Head of the universal church.
