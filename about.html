ABOUT APOSTLE ALFRED MAKHAVHU

Apostle Makhavhu was born in 1965 in Diepkloof, Soweto in a family of six children. He married Bongi Makhavhu and is blessed with two sons.

As a faithful servant of the Lord, he strives to establish order, excellence and integrity in the Body of Christ. His accomplishments achieved in the spreading of God's Word are indicative of his growth and contribution to the Kingdom of God.

Apostle Makhavhu was ordained in 1988 at FaithWays Bible Church, where he served as a youth pastor. He was called to fulltime ministry in 1995 and founded Flames of Fire Ministries in Rockville Soweto. Under his ministry the church grew and in 2001, he established a branch of Flames of Fire in Zakariyya Park.

In 2004, he established the Christ Kingdom Missions which seeks to bring leaders from various denominations together in the Body of Christ to fight the common enemy and speak with one voice. In 2005, he established a Flames of Fire Bible School which is an extension of Teamwork Bible College International (TBCI), recognized and accredited by the Accrediting Commission International (ACI).

STATEMENT OF FAITH

The foundational beliefs that guide our church and ministry

One God in Three Persons

We believe in one God, Creator of all things, existing in three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ - God the Son

We believe that Jesus Christ is God the Son, born of the virgin, died on the cross for our sins and rose again.

The Second Birth

We believe in the second birth through the Holy Spirit.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the Word of God and all Scriptures, both Old and New Testament are inspired by God.

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ to the earth as He promised.

Christ as Head of the Church

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Head of the universal church.
