Flames of Fire Ministries is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the light of the Gospel. Founded by Apostle Alfred Makhavhu, our mission is to ignite passion for God and service to others in the heart of our community.

Founder & Visionary

LEADERSHIP

Apostle Alfred Makhavhu

Apostle Alfred Makhavhu was born in 1965 in Diepkloof, Soweto. A faithful servant of the Lord, he strives to establish order, excellence, and integrity in the Body of Christ. He is married to Bongi Makhavhu and is blessed with two sons.

His journey began in 1988 at FaithWays Bible Church, where he was ordained and served as a youth pastor. Called to full-time ministry in 1995, he founded Flames of Fire Ministries in Rockville, Soweto. Under his leadership, the church grew and established the Zakariyya Park branch in 2001.

In 2004, he established Christ Kingdom Missions to promote unity within the Body of Christ. He also founded the Flames of Fire Bible School in 2005, an extension of Teamwork Bible College International (TBCI) accredited by the Accrediting Commission International (ACI).

WHAT WE BELIEVE
