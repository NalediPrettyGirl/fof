Sermon December 15, 2023

Walking in God's Purpose: Discovering Your Divine Destiny

Apostle Alfred Makhavhu

Senior Pastor

In today's world, many believers struggle with understanding their purpose in God's kingdom. We often find ourselves asking, "What is God's plan for my life?" This question has echoed through the hearts of countless individuals who desire to live a life that honors God and fulfills His divine purpose.

The Bible tells us in Jeremiah 29:11, "For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, to give you hope and a future." This verse reminds us that God has a specific plan for each of our lives, and it's our responsibility to seek Him and discover what that plan entails.

Walking in God's purpose requires three essential elements: faith, obedience, and patience. Faith allows us to trust in God's plan even when we cannot see the full picture. Obedience ensures that we follow His guidance even when it challenges our comfort zones. Patience helps us wait for His timing, understanding that God's timing is always perfect.

As we journey together in discovering our divine destiny, remember that God has equipped each of us with unique gifts and talents. These are not coincidental but are specifically designed to fulfill His purpose in our lives. When we align our lives with His will, we experience the joy and fulfillment that comes from living in our divine calling.

I encourage you to spend time in prayer and meditation, asking God to reveal His purpose for your life. Trust in His guidance, and step boldly into the destiny He has prepared for you.

Devotional December 12, 2023

The Power of Prayer in Daily Life

Pastor Bongi Makhavhu

Co-Pastor

Prayer is not just a religious ritual; it's a powerful communication tool that connects us directly with our Heavenly Father. In our busy lives, we often forget the incredible privilege we have to speak with the Creator of the universe at any moment of the day.

When we incorporate prayer into our daily routine, we invite God's presence into every aspect of our lives. Whether we're facing challenges at work, dealing with family issues, or simply going about our daily tasks, prayer transforms our perspective and aligns our hearts with God's will.

Start your day with prayer, thanking God for His blessings and asking for His guidance. Throughout the day, offer quick prayers of gratitude, requests for wisdom, or simply acknowledge His presence. End your day by reflecting on His goodness and surrendering your concerns to Him.

Remember, prayer is not about using fancy words or long speeches. God desires authentic communication from the heart. He knows your needs before you ask, but He delights in hearing from His children.

Make prayer a natural part of your daily life, and watch how God transforms your heart, your circumstances, and your relationship with Him.

Community December 10, 2023

Youth Ministry Outreach Success

Youth Ministry Team

Community Outreach

We are thrilled to share the incredible success of our recent youth outreach program that took place in our local community. Over the past month, our dedicated youth ministry team reached out to more than 200 young people, sharing the message of hope, love, and salvation through Jesus Christ.

The outreach program included various activities such as sports tournaments, music workshops, mentorship sessions, and interactive Bible studies. Our goal was not just to preach but to connect with young people where they are and show them the practical love of Christ through our actions.

One of the most impactful moments was when 15 young people made the decision to accept Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior during our final gathering. The joy and transformation we witnessed in their lives reminded us why this ministry is so important.

We also established ongoing mentorship relationships with many of the participants, ensuring that the seeds planted during this outreach will continue to grow. Our youth leaders are committed to walking alongside these young people as they begin their journey of faith.

We thank God for His faithfulness and ask for your continued prayers as we expand our youth outreach efforts. Together, we can make a lasting impact on the next generation for the Kingdom of God.
